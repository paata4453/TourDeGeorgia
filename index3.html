Tours

Tbilisi

Tbilisi is the beating heart of Georgia — old, modern, colorful, and full of life. Its narrow streets, wooden balconies, and courtyards reveal centuries of stories. The sulfur baths, perched below Narikala Fortress, offer warmth and tradition. Rustaveli Avenue and Freedom Square showcase art, culture, and Georgian spirit. The blend of Eastern charm and European energy makes it truly unforgettable. Wine bars, street art, and cozy cafés create a perfect urban experience. Every corner of Tbilisi invites you to slow down and explore with your senses. You’ll find history, hospitality, and soul wrapped into one city. It’s a perfect base to begin your Georgian adventure in comfort and wonder. Aleksi can guide you through Tbilisi’s famous sights and hidden treasures.

Batumi

Batumi shines on the Black Sea coast with a mix of modern style and warm hospitality. Its beaches, palm-lined boulevard, and dancing fountains attract visitors year-round. The Alphabet Tower and Ferris wheel light up the skyline with charm and fun. Stroll through the Botanical Garden for breathtaking sea and mountain views. Old Batumi has colorful buildings, street art, and cozy local cafés to enjoy. Nightlife here is vibrant, and the summer vibe is relaxing and joyful. The city is full of contrasts — old charm meets futuristic design at every step. It’s great for families, couples, and solo travelers looking for variety and peace. Whether you want sun, culture, or cuisine, Batumi offers it all in one place. Aleksi will bring you to Batumi comfortably so you enjoy every moment there.

Kazbegi

Kazbegi is Georgia’s highland jewel, where nature and silence speak loudest. The iconic Gergeti Trinity Church stands proud beneath majestic Mount Kazbek. It’s one of the most photographed places in Georgia — and for good reason. The mountain air is fresh, the skies vast, and the scenery absolutely breathtaking. You’ll find waterfalls, hiking trails, and hidden valleys perfect for exploration. The town of Stepantsminda offers local guesthouses and warm Svan hospitality. This is a place where legends live in the mountains and peace surrounds you. Ideal for nature lovers, adventurers, or anyone needing time away from noise. Even the road there — the Georgian Military Highway — is a scenic wonder. Aleksi will take you safely into the mountains for this unforgettable journey.

Sighnaghi

Sighnaghi is a romantic hilltop town, known as the “City of Love.” Its cobblestone streets and red-roofed houses feel like a European fairytale. Panoramic views of the Alazani Valley stretch endlessly beneath the town walls. You’ll find local wine cellars, art museums, and charming balconies everywhere. Sighnaghi is quiet, warm, and full of Kakhetian wine-making tradition. It’s the perfect getaway for couples or anyone seeking peace and beauty. The air smells like flowers, grapes, and slow village life — all at once. This region is the soul of Georgian wine and ancient hospitality. Sunsets here paint the sky in soft pastels, perfect for memory-making. Aleksi can take you there for a gentle, scenic, and wine-filled experience.

Mtskheta

Mtskheta is one of Georgia’s oldest and holiest cities, a true spiritual center. It was the ancient capital and still holds deep religious significance today. Here stands Svetitskhoveli Cathedral, a UNESCO World Heritage treasure. Jvari Monastery overlooks the town from a hill with sweeping views below. The city feels calm, peaceful, and filled with centuries of Georgian soul. It’s a small town, but every corner holds meaning, prayer, and legend. Traditional bakeries and little shops line its narrow cobbled streets. Perfect for a half-day trip to feel the sacred rhythm of the past. You’ll leave with quiet thoughts and a warm heart. Aleksi will take you there smoothly so you can focus on the beauty around you.

svaneti

Svaneti is a wild, mountainous region where history and nature stand untouched. Medieval Svan towers rise above ancient stone villages like something from legend. The air is crisp, the rivers clear, and snow-capped peaks surround you everywhere. Mestia and Ushguli offer deep cultural traditions and stunning alpine views. You can hike glaciers, visit local museums, and taste hearty mountain dishes. This is where Georgians preserve their most unique dialects and customs. Every house seems to tell a story, and every path leads to something sacred. It’s raw, remote, and deeply authentic — a dream for explorers and photographers. Even the road to Svaneti feels like a step back in time and up into the clouds. Aleksi will take you safely to this breathtaking mountain world with care and comfort.

Borjomi

Borjomi is Georgia’s famous spa town, known for its mineral water and green parks. The Borjomi-Kharagauli National Park surrounds the town in fresh pine air and trails. Take the cable car, explore the Romanov Palace, or relax by the mineral springs. It’s a quiet town perfect for healing, rest, and walks in nature. The mineral water is legendary — people come from all over to drink it fresh. Borjomi is clean, calm, and surrounded by forested mountains on all sides. Great for wellness seekers, families, and lovers of slow travel and silence. You can enjoy small cafés, local products, and peaceful mountain scenery. It’s one of the greenest and most refreshing places in the country. Aleksi will take you there so you can unwind and enjoy nature’s quiet power.

Vardzia

Vardzia is a 12th-century cave monastery carved into the rock of southern Georgia. It stretches across the cliffside with tunnels, churches, and secret passageways. Inside, you’ll find ancient frescoes, rooms, and stunning views over the valley. This place once held over 2,000 monks — it’s like a lost city in the mountains. It’s both peaceful and powerful, wrapped in silence and stone. The surrounding area is full of cliffs, rivers, and wide open skies. Great for those who love history, mystery, and off-the-path exploration. Nearby, you can also visit hot springs, castles, and green valleys. The entire region feels like a secret waiting to be discovered. Aleksi can take you to Vardzia and let you explore this sacred wonder in peace.

bakuriani

Bakuriani is a beloved resort town, famous for both winter and summer adventures. In winter, it’s full of skiers, snowboarders, and cozy fireside memories. In summer, it’s green, peaceful, and ideal for hiking and family picnics. The clean air and cool forests make it perfect for rest and health. It’s a great place for children with sledding hills, bike paths, and safe play areas. You’ll find modern hotels, charming cottages, and friendly locals everywhere. The town is surrounded by scenic mountain trails and quiet alpine lakes. Every season has its beauty — snowflakes or wildflowers, take your pick. People come here to play, heal, and breathe deeply. Aleksi can take you to Bakuriani and wait while you enjoy every season’s gift.

Qintsvisi monastery

Qintsvisi Monastery lies hidden in the forested hills of Shida Kartli. Its red-brick church dates back to the 12th century and holds rare, sacred frescoes. Surrounded by trees and birdsong, it’s one of Georgia’s quietest holy places. Inside the small church, Christ and angels are painted in deep colors and ancient style. The journey through the woods adds a feeling of peace and discovery. It’s ideal for spiritual seekers, history lovers, and quiet souls. Few crowds come here — you’ll often have the place to yourself. The landscape around is untouched, pure, and full of gentle light. It’s a powerful reminder of Georgia’s deep religious history and artistic soul. Aleksi will drive you through the forest paths straight to this timeless retreat.
